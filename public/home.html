ABOUT

Music Scheduler is a practice helper for professional and amateur musicians. With the help of Music Scheduler, you can keep track of your schedule and goals as a musician. Music Scheduler allows you to keep track of your daily tasks as you practice. You can log your practice hours, practice regularity and save links or insights from your session. Through regularly using Music Scheduler, you will have a better grasp on your goals and achievements as a musician. Music Scheduler will also remeber your past sessions so that you can compare your results to previous weeks and track your progress. and set goals for future weeks.
